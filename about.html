About Us

Meet the Creator

Troy Rojo, 19, is the creator of the Dog Breeds Encyclopedia. With a passion for animals and a deep interest in technology, Troy created this website to help dog lovers worldwide understand and appreciate various dog breeds.

His goal is to make reliable and engaging information about different dog breeds easily accessible to everyone. By sharing details on each breed's characteristics, temperament, and care requirements, he hopes to help current and future dog owners make informed decisions and deepen their bond with their pets.

This website aims to be a valuable resource for anyone looking to adopt, learn more about dog breeds, or simply enjoy discovering new and fascinating aspects of our furry friends. Troy believes that informed pet ownership leads to happier pets and families, and he is committed to growing this platform to support that mission.
