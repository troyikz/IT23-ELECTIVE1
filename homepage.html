Dogie pedia

Bulldog

The Bulldog is a muscular and hefty dog with a distinctive wrinkled face. Known for their gentle temperament, Bulldogs make great companions for families.

Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are friendly, intelligent, and devoted. They are known for their loyalty and are great family dogs, excelling in obedience and therapy work.

Poodle

Poodles are known for their curly coat and high intelligence. They come in different sizes and are often seen as excellent therapy dogs due to their temperament.

Beagle

The Beagle is a small to medium-sized breed known for its keen sense of smell and love of exploration. They are friendly, curious, and great with kids.

German Shepherd

German Shepherds are courageous, confident, and versatile dogs. They are often used in police work, search-and-rescue, and as loyal family pets.

Dachshund

Dachshunds are small but confident dogs known for their long bodies and short legs. They are lively and enjoy digging and exploring.
